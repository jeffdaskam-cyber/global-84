What the cohort can do inside Global 84.

The app brings the practical pieces of the international experience into one place: communication, discovery, events, team coordination, media, photos, and personal travel support.

Explore

Events

Designed to hand forward

A more coordinated, memorable, and scalable cohort experience.

Global 84 is built around Cohort 84's Singapore and Vietnam experience, but the structure is designed to be reused. Future cohorts can refresh the identity, destinations, imagery, admins, access rules, Explore content, currency tools, and launch materials without rebuilding the concept from scratch.
