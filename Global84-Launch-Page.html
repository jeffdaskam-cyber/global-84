Benefits

More than a website. It becomes the operating layer for the cohort experience.

Global 84 is useful because it reduces friction. People know where to look, how to coordinate, and how to keep the group experience feeling shared rather than fragmented.

A cohort app should feel like part concierge, part command center, and part memory book.

This design direction positions Global 84 as a polished digital companion for international programs, with enough structure for organizers and enough warmth for students.

Stronger coordination Events, chat, and shared updates give the cohort a common rhythm before and during travel.
Better sense of place Destination-specific features make the app feel tied to the program experience rather than generic.
Cleaner team collaboration Private team spaces let smaller groups work without losing connection to the larger cohort.
More memorable experience Photos, media, and shared conversation help preserve the program as a living narrative.

Login Process

Simple access for approved DU users.

The sign-in experience is intentionally straightforward. Users enter their University of Denver email address, receive a secure email link, and open that link to access the app.

What users see

Email-link sign in

The app accepts DU email addresses and sends a one-time sign-in link. That keeps the process lightweight while still limiting access to the intended user group.

Demo
name@du.edu
DU email required (`@du.edu`). After clicking the email link, the user returns to the app already authenticated.
How it works
1
Enter a DU email address The user starts with a University of Denver email account in the format `name@du.edu`.
2
Check the inbox The app sends a secure sign-in link directly to that email address.
3
Open the link to finish access Clicking the email link completes authentication and brings the user into the app experience.
